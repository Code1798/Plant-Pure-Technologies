About Phytoremediation

Phytoremediation is an eco-friendly water treatment technique that uses plants to remove heavy metals, toxins, and excess nutrients from polluted water.


Plant Pure Technologies develops floating treatment wetlands and sustainable plant systems to clean rivers, lakes, ponds, and industrial wastewater naturally.
